Education

How I got here

I have gotten where I am today with the help of many incredible classes, mentors, and friends. There are many sides to photography besides the actual taking of the pictures that people don't always realize. My college courses have helped a lot with the business and professionalism side of things. Below I have included just a few examples.

    Brigham Young University
    • MCom - helped with contract building and client correspondance
    • Marketing - taught me about social media and how to put myself on the map
    • Accounting - Helped me figure out how to start a business and keep all my financial books in line correctly

I have also had the great opportunity to work with some amazing photographers who have paved the way for me. I started by watching some educational videos by Marina Williams, and moved into being a second shooter for different photographers to research different styles, techniques, and niches of photography. Luckily, so many photographers focus on an environment of support and community instead of competition.

Finally, my biggest teacher in this business is practice! I love being creative and getting out of my comfort zone. Not every shot, or even every photoshoot, will always work out but it is important to push yourself so you can continue to grow and find your passion! I started my photography journey about two years ago and can definitely say that practice has been the single best thing for my growth!
