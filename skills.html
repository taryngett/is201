Skills

My favorite parts of my job

My skills present themselves in my photos, but are motivated by my love for people. I absolutely love telling a story through my photography, which is why I focus on candid moments more than poses for most of my clients. This helps open up my clientele because some people are uncomfortable in front of the camera. Doing prompts instead of poses really helps everyone loosen up and also lets natural personalities shine through!

I love involving props in my work which adds a creative touch. I have used plants, fabrics, glass, food, and many other things. Don't be afriad to ask about incorporating props to set your shoot apart!

Like many photographers, I have developed my own editing style. I use my own presets that I have created whenever I edit and never use filters. This helps my clients get a feel for my style and know what they are signing up for when they book with me.
